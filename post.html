Products & Services

Services

NVPS has developed and registered various ASME Section IX weld procedures that include:

  • Austenitic Stainless Steel Grades i.e. SS304/316L
  • Nickel/Moly Grades i.e. HAST-C276
  • Duplex and SuperDuplex Grades i.e. UNS31803

With over 25 years experience in the industry, our highly technical staff and manufacturing team continually provide innovative solutions to any in-process design changes. Our fabrication capabilities are continually expanding; please see our photo gallery for images of recently completed projects.

We strive to consistently exceed our customers project expectations with product innovation, quality, service and value.

Certificates

NVPS is certified in accordance with CSA Standard B51, Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Pressure Piping Code(s):

  • ASME B31.1 Power Piping
  • ASME B31.3 Process Piping
  • CWB Certification in accordance with CSA Standard W47.1, Division 3
